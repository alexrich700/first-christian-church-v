Welcome To First Christian Church

What to Expect

  • You will find a congregation that values tradition but is very relevant for today.
  • You will find a church that reads Scripture aloud, not just a sentence or two, because we believe that people are transformed by hearing God’s Word.
  • Our Communion Table is open to all who believe in Jesus Christ as Savior, regardless of denomination.
  • You will see a church that works to find what we have in common in Christ to help you build your relationship with God. Many come from different denominational backgrounds and find a home here because of this.
  • You will find a church that will take you where you are and help to guide you to where God desires you to be.

What We Believe

What We Believe

We hold strongly to the belief that the Bible is the only authoritative guide for a Christian's life.

Church Mission

We are here to bring you into a closer relationship with the Lord through Christian fellowship, prayer, and Biblical teachings.

We Come To Serve

First Christian Church supports several local charities to help those in need.

Church Community

First Christian Church believes that as a Church we are one body, and we must act like it in love, mercy, and thanksgiving.

What We Believe

  • We believe in the authority of the Word of God in our daily lives.
  • We believe that a relationship with God through Jesus Christ is vital.
  • We believe that church is not a building but consists of all those who know Jesus Christ as their personal Lord and Savior.
  • We believe and are called to gather around the Lord’s Table in Communion weekly as a remembrance of His great love for us until He returns.
  • We share Christ’s call and mission to be a global church and send the message around the globe.

Still Have Questions?

What can I wear to church?

We have people come to church wearing jeans to suits, t-shirts to dresses. We just want you in church.

What if I have children?

We have a fully equipped children’s ministry program with current and former teachers in charge. We have a regular children’s time during the service as well as activities for the children.

I am new here; will I be welcome?

Absolutely, we will welcome you but not draw unwanted attention to you.

I grew up in a different church, will I be welcome?

We seek all who want to know Jesus Christ greater and walk with Him regardless of previous church affiliation.

My last experience with “church” was bad… What makes you different?

At First Christian Church, we seek to build people up and remind them of the simple truth, that God loves you!
